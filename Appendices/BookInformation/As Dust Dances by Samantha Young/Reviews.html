May 26th 2021, review by Kierra:

4 stars

I am happy that I randomly read Samantha Young’s first Play On novel. I couldn’t wait to read this one to see if I would like her writing. I do! I think she does an excellent job at capturing the emotions of characters, leaves you wanting to know more about them. I love a good healing journey and you can tell Young took her time to develop good stories for the characters. I was surprised in one part and then it became predictable towards the end. I didn’t mind it though because the story started with shattered pieces and Young pulled it all together in the end.
