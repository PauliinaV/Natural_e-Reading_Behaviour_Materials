I'm having a hard time rating this one. There were parts that I liked and parts that I hated. Some of the technical parts were a little heavy and I found myself skimming them, but other explanations I enjoyed. Sometimes I liked Marcus and sometimes he drove me nuts. Overall, it was a good story and interesting to ponder if it could ever come true. I liked it enough that I'm going to try the second one, but I hope the characters have a bit more depth to them in the second book.
