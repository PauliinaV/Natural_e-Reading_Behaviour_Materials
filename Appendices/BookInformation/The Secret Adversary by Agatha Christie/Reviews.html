August 21st 2021, review by Mwrogers:

3 stars

This was a fun, quick read. It was written in the early 1920s and the slang of that time…. “old thing” “chew a bun” “cat in a rage” “put me wise” “you’re so darn plucky”….was very distracting to me. Kind of irritating. It was like those old Katherine Hepburn movies where they talk a mile a minute. But still, it was a good story and I kept going back and forth as to who Mr Brown was and every time I was sure, I would change my mind! Christie always tricks me! This was no exception.
