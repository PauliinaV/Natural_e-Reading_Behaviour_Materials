July 25th 2021, review by Jesus:

3.7 stars

Sooo yeah... it was aight I guess? As usual with Brandon the world building is nice and there and some interesting characters. Two characters I was disappointed in the direction they were written. Two main sisters honestly kinda boring.

It was definitely a fun read! I was just disappointed in the direction many things went (characters, villains etc..) as well as not liking the pacing at all. First 30% was super slow then out of nowhere things picked up (The Sanderson Avalanche Effect). Loved how well everything was moving past 30%...but towards the last 75-90% it just felt soooo rushed.
